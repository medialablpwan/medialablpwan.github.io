Designing the future of IoT.

MediaLab LPWAN is an open workgroup to collaborate creating and testing the new generation of IoT networks and devices.

Join Us On GitHub

An open workgroup for everyone.

We prototype, design and research based on the most known development boards (Arduino, ESP32, STM32...), promoting easy access to anyone who wants to get started in IoT.

Latest Projects_

Water Level Sensor

In honour of Martin Cooper, MediaLab collaborator Daniel Rodríguez made distance sensor with a LilyGO board to monitor the water level of the river that flows through the campus of the EPI of Gijón.

Smart Buoy

Designed for sea solutions, smart buoy can get info of waves, for example height, temperature and positioning.

TTN Tutorial

Uploaded to our GitHub page, a full tutorial about node programming and connecting to TTN, as well as the deployment of an IoT data processing full stack device.

Team Meetings

Every two weeks, we have a meeting for updating our projects, discover new makers, exchange knowledge, discuss solutions and propouse new challenges.

Collaborative Projects

Our key are the people thinking for the people. We use our free time to research, explore, try and break hardware and code new things. With the only concern that we are going forward. Because some big projects started by exploring crazy things, and we know that, in few years, all we know will be connecting us.

Community Synergies

With the support of city hall of Gijón and the Asturian goberment autorities, we are growing the Iot culture, opening an widening area for innovation companies and startups.
